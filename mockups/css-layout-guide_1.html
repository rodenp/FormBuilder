6. Direction & Flow

Flexbox: flex-direction

Items have different sizes so you can see the flow direction clearly

Row (default)
flex-direction: row
1
2 wide
3 tall
4

Left → Right

Row Reverse
flex-direction: row-reverse
1
2 wide
3 tall
4

Right → Left (1 is now on the right)

Column
flex-direction: column
1 short
2 wider
3 widest item

Top → Bottom

Column Reverse
flex-direction: column-reverse
1 short
2 wider
3 widest item

Bottom → Top (1 is now at the bottom)

⚠️ Important: When you use flex-direction: column, the meaning of justify-content and align-items swap!

Row: justify = left/right, align = up/down
Column: justify = up/down, align = left/right

Grid: grid-auto-flow

✓ Grid is simpler: Unlike flexbox, changing the flow does NOT swap what justify and align do.

justify-* = always horizontal (left/right)
align-* = always vertical (up/down)

7. Cheat Sheet

What you want Flexbox Grid
Move items left/right justify-content justify-content or justify-items
Move items up/down align-items align-content or align-items
Left / Top flex-start start
Center center center
Right / Bottom flex-end end
Stretch height align-items: stretch (default) align-items: stretch (default)
Stretch width flex-grow: 1 justify-items: stretch (default)
Equal sizes flex: 1 on all items 1fr 1fr 1fr
Change direction flex-direction: column grid-auto-flow: column
Perfect center justify-content: center;
align-items: center;		 place-items: center;
Remember:

Flexbox = one-dimensional (row OR column)
Grid = two-dimensional (rows AND columns at once)
